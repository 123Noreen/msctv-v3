Our Journey of Growth and Progress

Maris Stella College Thimbirigaskatuwa is a relatively new, vibrant and rapidly progressing educational institute. On the 1 st of January 2004, on the site of the former Catholic Teacher-training College in Bolawalana, Sri Lanka, the School began as a Branch of Maris Stella College Negombo, motivating young souls to broaden and deepen their human development and bring honor and glory of our society. A major milestone in the history of Maris Stella College Branch school was reached in 2014. Our Alma Mater moved from Bolawalana to a brand-new home in Thimbirigaskatuwa.
